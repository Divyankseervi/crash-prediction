Understanding the Data Imbalance
Why SMOTE is critical for this dataset

The dataset exhibits extreme class imbalance — 90.7% of cases are classified as POD (Property Damage Only), while Moderate and Serious injuries together account for less than 2.4% of all records.

This imbalance means naive models achieve ~91% accuracy by simply predicting POD for every case. SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique) generates synthetic minority-class samples so models can learn to distinguish all severity levels.
